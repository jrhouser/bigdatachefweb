How the algorithm works (in plain english)

- Choose a random recipe from collection

- Randomly pick ingredients from recipe.

- From those chosen ingredients pick related ingredients that is connected through the graph.

For instance, if there are olives in our origional recipe the app might pick venison and/or paprika as an additional ingredient (see zoom in of co-occurance network in the below figure).

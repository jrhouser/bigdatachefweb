Chocolate chip crispy brittles

Orange portuguese style shrimp on bed of zucchini salad

Note: The recipe here is given as gluten free but regular flour can be used for those who aren’t gluten challenged. Also Kellog's no longer sells gluten free rice krisipes. However, alternatives can be found here.

Ingredients

amount ingredient
3/4 cup butter (softened)
1 1/4 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1.5 cups rice flour
3/4 cups tapioca starch
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
2 cups choclate chips
4 cups Gluten free crispy rice cereal
3 cups mini marshmellows

Directions

- To a large bowl mix softened butter, flour, salt, and brown sugar.

- Beat in the egg.

- Add remainder of ingredients

- Bake at 375 for 8-12 min or until lightly brown on top.
